WEMBLEY Stadium

Welcome to Wembley Stadium

Wembley Stadium is the iconic home of English football and one of the most famous sports venues in the world. Located in London, it has a seating capacity of 90,000, making it the largest stadium in the UK and the second-largest in Europe. Opened in 2007, the modern Wembley is renowned for its striking design, including the 133-meter-high arch that dominates the skyline. The stadium hosts major football matches, including the FA Cup Final and international games, as well as world-class concerts and events.
